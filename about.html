Our Story

Architecture
with purpose.

Studio Arc was founded in 2008 with a simple belief: great architecture is not just about beautiful buildings — it's about how those buildings make people feel. We are an interdisciplinary studio working at the intersection of architecture, interior design, and urban thinking.

Who We Are

A studio built on curiosity.

We are a team of 14 architects, designers, and researchers united by a passion for places that matter. Our practice spans residential, commercial, cultural, and hospitality projects — each approached with the same rigour and care.

We believe in slow architecture: listening before designing, questioning before building, and always asking who this space is truly for.

What Drives Us

Our Values

01

Context First

Every site has a story. We listen to the land, the culture, and the community before drawing a single line.

02

Timeless Over Trendy

We design buildings meant to age gracefully — spaces that feel right in 10 years, not just at completion.

03

Sustainable by Default

Environmental responsibility is not an add-on. It is embedded in every material choice, orientation, and detail.

04

Collaborative Process

Our best work happens when clients, engineers, craftspeople, and designers think together from day one.

05

Detail at Every Scale

From urban masterplans to door handles, we believe the quality of the whole lives in the quality of each part.

06

Joy in Making

We take our work seriously but never lose sight of delight — the pleasure of a well-proportioned room, a shaft of light.
